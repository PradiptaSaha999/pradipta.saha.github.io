About Me

I am Pradipta Saha, a passionate Electrical and Electronics Engineer with a strong interest in Automation, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence.

I have earned my Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET) and I have more than seven years of experience in the Automation sector. Throughout my career, I have worked with leading organizations such as Siemens, ABB, where I led commissioning teams and worked on industrial automation system and substartion automation solutions up to 400kV level. Currently I am working as Assistant Manager Electrical and automation in Syngenta Bangladesh Ltd.

During my undergraduate studies, I was deeply involved in various research projects on robotics, computer vision, biomedical engineering, and embedded systems with several publications in international conferences. These experiences have shaped my interest in robotics and AI, particularly in developing intelligent, adaptive systems for real-world applications.

Alongside my studies, I actively contributed to student organizations such as IEEE Society CUET Branch and Robo Mechatronics Association, where I gained hands-on experience in robotics and embedded systems.
